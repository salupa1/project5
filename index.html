Mr. Beast Survive and Win Challenges

$10,000 Every Day You Survive In The Wilderness

This video is about two strangers, Hayes and Grizz, who are dropped in the wilderness by MrBeast. They are given $10,000 every day they survive. The challenge is to see how long they can last. In the beginning, they are excited and optimistic. They work together to find food and shelter. They build a fire and gather supplies. As time goes on, the challenge becomes more difficult. They run out of food and have to find new sources. They argue with each other and Hayes gets frustrated. At one point, Hayes even steals Grizz's flare gun, which they can use to end the challenge and go home. Grizz convinces Hayes to stay, and they eventually decide to work together as a team. They build a more permanent shelter and learn to fish. After 74 days, they decide to end the challenge and go home. They have won $670,000.

$10,000 Every Day You Survive In A Grocery Store

A man named Alex is challenged to live inside a grocery store for as long as possible. For every day he manages to stay, he earns $10,000. Alex must sell $10,000 worth of products from the store to the host, Jimmy, who also owns the store. Throughout his stay, Alex encounters various challenges including power outages, the need to build a shelter, operating a forklift, and emotional moments like a visit from his family. Despite the hardships, Alex innovates by creating a shower and laundry area using items from the store.After 45 days, Alex decides to leave the store, walking away with a total of $450,000.

Last To Leave Circle Wins $500,000

The video is about a competition where 100 people compete for a prize of $500,000. The last person standing in a circle wins the prize. The contestants are not allowed to leave the circle or touch a red line around it. If they do, they are eliminated. The video is divided into two parts. In the first part, the circle slowly shrinks over time and the contestants are challenged with various tasks to get them out of the circle. For example, they have to play rock paper scissors, dodge a soccer ball, and answer trivia questions.

Survive 100 Days Trapped, Win $500,000

The video is about two strangers, Susie and Bailey, who are locked in an isolation chamber for 100 days. If they can survive the entire time, they will split $500,000.
